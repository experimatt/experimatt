Experiments

This is a list of all my hair brained ideas and experiments over the years. Many failed, some succeeded, but I learned something from every single one of them. The list is perpetually growing.
  • Invisible Network: An anonymous job search platform
  • OMG Transit: A real-time transit app, started at a hackathon
  • Mpls Jr Devs: A community for aspiring and less experienced software engineers
  • Junior Dev Salary Survey: To evaluate how demographics affect junior dev salary expectations
  • Break The Bubble: An event series to help Minnesotans make new friends
  • Why you should quit your job: An ignite talk inspiring people to quit their jobs
  • Early Lunch Club: A website that tells if you if it's time to eat lunch
  • Hiring Exchange: An event to connect job seekers and employers in real-time at Minnebar
  • No Recruiters Allowed: An event where developers met other developers over beers
  • Frozen pizza tasting, cheese edition: A blind frozen pizza tasting experiment
